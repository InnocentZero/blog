Curb Your Demons

A battle with the demons within us.

July 4, 2023 ◦ 1 min ◦
  • #poems
  • #literature

Will it bleed,

Will it crawl?

Will it plead

As it falls?


Will it wail,

Will it cry?

Will it fail

As it dies?


Will I heed

To its call?

Will I need

That it falls?


Will I sail

Beneath the sky?

Will I hail

That it dies?
