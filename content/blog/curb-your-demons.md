A battle with the demons within us.

<!-- more -->

Will it bleed,

Will it crawl?

Will it plead

As it falls?

<br>
Will it wail,

Will it cry?

Will it fail

As it dies?

<br>
Will I heed

To its call?

Will I need

That it falls?

<br>
Will I sail

Beneath the sky?

Will I hail

That it dies?
